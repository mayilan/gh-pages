Mayilan Krishnamoorthy

Visuals are a powerful way to not only understand but to remember complex concepts. To visualize, is to form a mental image of something that is not directly available. Visualist idealizes thinking and communicating, visually. Having worked with visuals for a good amount of time, I understand the impact and benefits of communicating through visuals. Visuals poke prod and makes us think, sometimes beyond what the author intended.

We are all laypersons in fields that we are not familiar with. With that thought in mind, these pages are an expression of the richness and complexity simple concepts. Hope you have fun exploring them as much as I had creating them.
